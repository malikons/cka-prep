ingredients:
============

- oat milk - 25ml
- coffee - 1 capsule

apparatus:
==========

- nespresso machine
- milk frother

Instructions:
=============

- add milk in frother
- place a capsule in nespresso machine
- put a coffee mug under the machine
- press the coffee button on the machine to get coffee in the mug
- turn on the frother to froth milk
  - wait 15 seconds
  - stop the frother
  - pour the milk in the mug
- stir the coffee
